Quadratic Practice: Midpoint Method

Any two solutions to a quadratic equation are symmetrically spaced around a midpoint. For an equation in the form:

x² + Bx + C = 0

The midpoint is: m = -B / 2

The roots are: m - u and m + u

Since the product of the roots is C:

(m - u)(m + u) = C

m² - u² = C
